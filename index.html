Brand Introduction
Skin & Mind, a skin care product created by a Taiwanese biotech team, was designed especially for modern metropolitan women. They extract Chinese herbal extraction in order to repair women’s tired skin and mind.
Project Objective
· Deliver the principle of the brand – natural cleanliness – and strengthen brand awareness.
· Enhance brand trust and product reliability by recommendations from amateurs.
Target Audience
· female/30-40 years old/service or financial industry
